Cosmic Civilization

A great civilization with super advanced technology and built structures that go beyond human understanding, since apparently each building they created gathered energy from ether or from the very air we breathe every day. What happened to them? Where did those intelligent buildings go? Where are all those years of history? Was it all buried? Did someone take care of altering history? If so, who, how, and with what purpose would someone bury such technological advances? Could these secrets be hidden in the Vatican?
Forgotten Empire maps

Who will have the history books? It seems as if an entire nation disappeared overnight, not just from the map, but also from the face of the earth. In several old Atlas books and lost journals, they describe Tartary as a great empire. Did we forget about that?

Underground city

Have you ever wondered what is beneath our feet, while we walk, while we go to work or to school? Have we been building on top of Tartaria for centuries? Every underground drilling in big cities reveals a layer of underground buildings.

Tunnels

Most of the tunnels we are using today are showing to have been discovered, not created by us. We're just using a discovery from a piece of Tartaria. Kilometer-long tunnels that were left to us as an inheritance for our trains and subways.
